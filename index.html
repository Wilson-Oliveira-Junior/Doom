Fodder

Weak and deal pitiful damage

Fodder is an enemy type in Doom Eternal, as well as its first and second dlc releases. They are weak and deal pitiful damage. They all take one fuel to chainsaw.

Heavy

Moderate amounts of damage

Heavy is an enemy type in Doom Eternal, and its first and second dlc releases. They have a medium-sized health pool and do moderate amounts of damage. They all take three fuel to chainsaw.

Super Heavy

All Super Heavies cannot be chainsawed

Super Heavy is an enemy type in Doom Eternal, as well as its first and second dlc releases. All Super Heavies cannot be chainsawed. Super Heavies are often very dangerous, with large health pools, and heavily damaging attacks.

The Gladiator

Infamous for the many lives it has claimed

The Gladiator is a demon that debuted in Doom Eternal. It serves as the boss of Sentinel Prime.
